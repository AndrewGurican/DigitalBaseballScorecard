Welcome to the DigitalBaseballScorecard.

This is an app where you can make and save a scorecard for your baseball team on your Ipad. There are many fields that you can edit and you can also load previous scorecards from the past. So if you want to stop messing with a big stack of paper scorecards, this app is for you.
